# CSS Classes

In my CSS Snippet, I've implemented a number of custom CSS classes to do various things.

Here's a basic note without any classes added:

<img width="2870" height="1886" alt="20260206 10 41 13@2x" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e0349ea7-c1db-451a-8102-57ba24abf440" />

### `hide-title`

Removes the built-in title element that matches the note's filename.

<img width="2870" height="1886" alt="20260206 10 42 29@2x" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/22f873ba-c136-4379-bcd2-0943b346c72e" />

### `justify`

Sets the text-align property of the note to 'justify'.

<img width="2870" height="1886" alt="20260206 10 43 54@2x" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d37055f2-d36e-4e23-8979-b71ac73d9a3c" />

### `bold-colored`

Colors strong or bold elements to be the vault's accent color.

<img width="2870" height="1886" alt="20260206 10 44 25@2x" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/48244a29-a134-4c6e-8eda-d575238c89d8" />

### `italic-colored`

Colors emphasized or italic elements to be the vault's *secondary* accent color.

<img width="2870" height="1886" alt="20260206 10 45 26@2x" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/14f1aa55-41d7-47da-90a2-4f91e0c65a75" />

### `beige-accent`

Sets the accent color of the note to a beige color.

> For the example image, `bold-colored` is also enabled.

<img width="2870" height="1886" alt="20260206 10 48 44@2x" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2897cf42-703f-4bc9-a805-fc5a928fae1e" />

### `full-width`

Lets the note take up the full width of the window.

<img width="2870" height="1886" alt="20260206 10 49 10@2x" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/682bf5dc-9e5b-4bd5-82aa-3b983e075fd6" />

### `cover-float`

If the first element of a note is an image, has it float to the right of the note content.

> 'Charm' is copyright 2024 of Clairo Records.

<img width="2870" height="1886" alt="20260206 10 49 34@2x" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7f5cbad3-5aea-40da-aadf-9796acf946bb" />

### `images-gallery-sized`

Sizes images to have a max-height and a faded background.

> 'Charm' is copyright 2024 of Clairo Records.
